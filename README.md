## Featured Product Case Studies

### DELIVER Nigeria App

A digital marketplace and analytics platform developed for the Global Alliance for Improved Nutrition to improve household-level visibility into vegetable purchase, consumption behavior, market linkages, and program targeting.

**Product focus:** Marketplace development, household onboarding, retailer onboarding, analytics pipeline, consumption dashboards, cohort analysis, field operations intelligence.

**Impact:** Improved the speed, accuracy, and usefulness of program monitoring data, reduced reliance on slow ad-hoc surveys, and enabled targeted interventions based on real user behavior.

Website: https://delivernigeria.org/
Repository: https://github.com/Nicholasekam/deliver-nigeria-app

### VPCart9

A subscription growth and user onboarding improvement project for VPCart, focused on improving adoption, reducing time-to-value, and strengthening the path from acquisition to paid subscription.

**Product focus:** Product roadmap ownership, onboarding optimization, UX improvement, checkout simplification, analytics instrumentation, acquisition campaigns, referral incentives.

**Impact:** Delivered improved subscriber growth, validated product-market fit improvements, and created a repeatable launch playbook for future product releases.

Website: https://www.vpcart.com
Repository: https://github.com/Nicholasekam/vpcart9

---

### Waste4Reward

A circular economy platform backed by The Coca-Cola Foundation, designed to incentivize, track, and monetize post-consumer plastic recovery through a digital marketplace and blockchain-enabled reward layer.

**Product focus:** Digital marketplace development, tokenized incentive design, collector onboarding, aggregator coordination, recycler integration, fintech/payment workflows, traceable supply-chain data.

**Impact:** Supported the recovery of over 25,000 metric tons of plastic waste, created a traceable informal collection supply chain, and enabled over $3 million in recovered material value.

Website: https://waste4reward.com/
Repository: https://github.com/Nicholasekam/waste4reward

---

### ShaktiHub App

A commercial field enablement and retailer marketplace platform developed to improve Unilever’s route-to-market operations, retailer visibility, stock access, ordering, payments, and sales performance tracking.

**Product focus:** Retailer marketplace, mobile-first onboarding, unified product database, inventory visibility, role-based access, sales officer dashboards, payment processing, change management.

**Impact:** Onboarded 13,000+ retailers and 190 sales officers, facilitated over $7 million in annual channel sales, and improved field productivity, order accuracy, and operational visibility.

Website: https://www.shaktihub.org/
Repository: https://github.com/Nicholasekam/shaktihub-app
